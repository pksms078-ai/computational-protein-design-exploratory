# Ethics & Responsible Use Statement

This project is released for scientific research, education, and exploratory purposes only.

## Ethical Principles
- Transparency over performance claims
- No overstated biological or medical outcomes
- No misuse for harmful biological engineering

## Safety Boundaries
- This framework does NOT design deployable biological agents
- No pathogen optimization
- No wet-lab synthesis instructions

## Intended Use
- Computational hypothesis generation
- Academic exploration
- Educational demonstrations
- Pre-experimental research guidance

## Prohibited Use
- Clinical decision-making
- Human or animal experimentation
- Weaponization or dual-use misuse

## Responsibility
The author does not claim experimental validation.
Users are responsible for complying with:
- Institutional ethics committees
- National and international biosafety regulations

## AI Ethics
- Model limitations are documented
- No black-box biological claims
- Emphasis on explainability and constraints
